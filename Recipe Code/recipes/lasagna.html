Homemade Lasagna

DESCRIPTION

Make delicious homemade lasagna in only a few steps!

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 Pound of ground meat
  • Canned Tomatoes both crushed and whole
  • Fresh Herbs to include parsley and garlic
  • Sugar
  • Spices and Seasoning to include dried basil, dried oregano, salt and pepper
  • Uncooked Noodles
  • Parmesan and Mozzarella Cheese
  • Eggs

STEPS

  1. Cook the meat until brown and crumbly. Add onion, tomato products, half of the parsley, garlic, basil, 1.5 teaspoon of salt, oregano and sugar.
  2. Cook the noodles by boiling the lasagna in lightly salted water
  3. To make the cheese layer mix cottage cheese, Parmesan cheese, eggs, the remaining parsley, the remaining salt and pepper in a bowl
  4. Layer the ingredients according to recipe (starting with the sauce and ending with mozzarella) until the lasagna is assembled
  5. Cover the lasagna with foil and bake in a preheated oven for about half an hour. Remove the foil and continue baking until the top is golden brown
  6. Bake in the oven for 30 to 40 minutes. Remove the foil and bake until cheese is golden brown, 5 to 10 minutes.
  7. Remove from the oven and let stand for 10 minutes before cutting and serving.
